Company

We use your information to process and fulfill orders, provide customer support, communicate updates and promotions, and improve our website and services. We may also use your information for fraud prevention, security monitoring, and compliance with applicable laws.

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted partners, including payment processors, shipping and logistics providers, and analytics/marketing partners, in order to provide and improve our services.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA), including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

Security

Our store is hosted on a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified platform with SSL encryption and industry-standard security practices to protect your personal information. Payment information is processed exclusively through certified providers. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your full credit-card details.

If you access our site from outside your home country, your information may be transferred and processed in other jurisdictions, including the United States, where data protection rules may differ from those in your country of residence.
